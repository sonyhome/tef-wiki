

# The Electric Fursuits
## Community Wiki

Hello and welcome to the community wiki of the Electric Fursuits group!
These Wiki pages are designed to allow us as a furry community to collaborate and contribute knowledge into one central space.
Here, you will find anything ranging from sewing tips and tricks, pattern making, basic electronics and lighting, all the way up
to advanced software stacks for LED mapping or rendering!

This Wiki is community-driven. [If you want to contribute](Contributing), don't be shy!
